Symptoms of Covid-19

If you suspect that you have Covid 19, we recommend that you go immediately to your nearest health center. Some symptoms that you could suffer are the following:

  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Tiredness
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Throat pain
  • Headache
  • Aches and pains
  • Diarrhea
  • Skin rash or discoloration of the fingers or toes
  • Red or irritated eyes

Serious symptoms are:

  • Shortness of breath or dyspnea
  • Loss of mobility or speech or feeling confused
  • Chest pain
